OUR HISTORY

Our story

The Windermere Brewing Company is a family owned brewery started by Arthur Barnes, a brew master from England. Barnes’s family settled in Ontario in 1935. Travelling from Britain, he and his brother brought with them their passion for brewing ales and lagers.

Windex today

WINDEX TODAY

History

After more than 85 years of making draft beers for pubs in the town on Hamilton, The Windermere Brewing Company has now entered the bottled beer business. Our brewery, located in Hamilton has started its first can and bottle packaging facility. Windex Beers from here are sent across Canada to those who prefer the taste of Classic English Beers. The new packaging plant can produce up to 200L of beer in a day. That’s quite a lot considering we make 250L of draughts everyday
